About Us

Empowering the AM Community

  • ✔ Acts as a central hub connecting businesses, researchers, and enthusiasts nationwide
  • ✔ Fosters innovation and collaboration within the additive manufacturing (AM) field
  • ✔ Encourages knowledge exchange across different industries and disciplines
  • ✔Supports a growing ecosystem of AM professionals and institutions

Engaging Events & Continuous Learning

  • ✔ Hosts annual networking events featuring global AM experts and innovators
  • ✔ Showcases the latest technologies and success stories in AM
  • ✔ Organizes monthly webinars on current trends and developments
  • ✔ Welcomes participation from industries, academia, and research institutions

We aim to be a driving force for:

knowledge

Knowledge Norwegian AM will stay informed and actively participate in the international developments within AM to ensure that Norwegian stakeholders have updated knowledge and experiences. The association will promote collaboration with similar organizations internationally.

Dissemination

Through communication and outreach, Norwegian AM aims to be a prominent actor with a clear voice and a comprehensive understanding of the AM community and activities in Norway. Norwegian AM will facilitate favorable frameworks and regulations for the technology in Norway.

Implementation

Take responsibility for organizing an annual AM conference, relevant workshops, and courses, as well as conducting awareness campaigns and ensuring participation in relevant forums. Showcase best practices to highlight the application areas of AM, digitalization, etc.

NCAM 2025

Stavanger, Norway. 24 March – 26 March, 2024

Join us for a transformative three-day experience at the scenic Fiskepiren in Stavanger! Bringing together leading industry experts, researchers, and passionate professionals, #NCAM2025 is a unique platform for exploring and advancing innovation in additive manufacturing.

NCAM 2024

Hammerfest, Norway. 11 March – 13 March, 2024

Venturing north to the world’s northernmost city, NCAM 2024 offered a unique blend of industry insights and Arctic adventure. Attendees explored the latest applications of additive manufacturing across various sectors, from aerospace to healthcare.

NCAM 2023

Trondheim, Norway. 20 March – 22 March, 2023

Hosted in Trondheim in March 2023, NCAM 2023 was a two-day conference packed with insights and networking opportunities. Organized in collaboration with NTNU and SINTEF, this event brought together over 19 speakers to explore the latest advancements and applications in additive manufacturing.
